Why Bayesian Methods?

Bayesian inference provides a principled way to combine:

  • Physics-based simulation outputs
  • Real-world measurements
  • Domain knowledge and uncertainty

The result is a calibrated model that doesn’t just fit the data — it understands the uncertainty around it. That leads to better predictions, safer extrapolation, and more robust engineering decisions.

How We Make Bayesian Optimization Work in Practice
